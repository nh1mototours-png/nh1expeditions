Umling La — World's Highest Road

Summit the Guinness World Record motorable road at 5,883m. 50% less oxygen. The ultimate biking achievement.

📏 ~1,500 km
📅 14–15 Days
☀️ Jun – Sep
🏔️ Max 5,883m (Umling La)

Day-by-Day Itinerary

The World Record Summit

⚠️ Extreme Altitude Warning — 5,883m

  • At 5,883m you have ~50% of sea-level oxygen. HACE/HAPE are real risks
  • Minimum 3-4 days acclimatization at 3,500m (Leh) before attempting
  • Diamox mandatory (consult your doctor). Carry emergency oxygen
  • ILP for Hanle/Umling La available ONLY at DC Office Leh — no online option
  • Temperature can drop to -15°C even in July. Full thermals required
  • BRO provides a "Certificate of World Record" at the summit board
▼
Day 1–3
Manali → Jispa → Sarchu → Leh | ~405 km
Standard Manali-Leh route. Jispa (3,100m) → Sarchu (4,253m) → Leh (3,500m). Baralacha La, Tanglang La. MANDATORY rest on arrival at Leh. Do NOT rush.
🏨 Jispa → Sarchu → Leh🛣️ ~405 km · 3 days
▼
Day 4
Leh Rest Day #1 | Critical
Full acclimatization. Light walking only. Collect SPECIAL ILP for Hanle/Umling La at DC Office — in person only, no online option. Takes a few hours.
🏨 Leh🏔️ 3,500m
▼
Day 5
Leh Rest Day #2 | Optional but Recommended
Additional acclimatization if you feel any AMS symptoms. Visit Leh Palace, Shanti Stupa. Your body NEEDS time before 5,883m. Better safe than sorry.
🏨 Leh🏔️ 3,500m
▼
Day 6
Leh → Hanle | ~260 km
South via Upshi, Chumathang hot springs, Nyoma. Rupshu Plateau. Hanle (4,500m) — Indian Astronomical Observatory (one of the world's highest). Overnight Hanle. Clear night sky like nowhere else.
🏨 Hanle🏔️ 4,500m🛣️ ~260 km · ~7 hrs
▼
Day 7
Hanle → UMLING LA SUMMIT → Hanle | ~120 km
THE DAY. Early morning start. Climb to Umling La (5,883m) — Guinness World Record motorable road. 50% less oxygen. Photos at the BRO summit board. Certificate from BRO. Do NOT stay at summit more than 20-30 min. Descend to Hanle. Celebrate responsibly.
🏨 Hanle🏔️ 5,883m (summit)🛣️ ~120 km · ~4-5 hrs return
▼
Day 8
Hanle → Tso Moriri | ~120 km
Scenic ride across Rupshu plateau to Tso Moriri (4,522m). Korzok Monastery. Migratory birds. Remote, sacred lake.
🏨 Korzok🏔️ 4,522m🛣️ ~120 km · ~5 hrs
▼
Day 9
Tso Moriri → Leh | ~220 km
Return to Leh. Tso Kar salt lake stop.
🏨 Leh🏔️ 3,500m🛣️ ~220 km · ~7 hrs
▼
Day 10–11
Nubra → Pangong Loop | ~400 km
Khardung La 5,359m. Nubra Valley & Turtuk. Shyok road to Pangong. Sunrise. Chang La 5,360m. Return Leh.
🏨 Nubra → Pangong → Leh🛣️ ~400 km · 2 days
▼
Day 12
Leh Rest + Monasteries | ~80 km
Hemis, Thiksey, Shey monasteries. Magnetic Hill. Gurudwara Pathar Sahib. Rest day before return.
🏨 Leh🏔️ 3,500m
▼
Day 13–15
Leh → Sarchu → Jispa → Manali | ~405 km
Return trip. Tanglang La, More Plains, Baralacha La. Sarchu, Jispa, Manali. Buffer day for weather/road conditions.
🏨 Sarchu → Jispa → Manali🛣️ ~405 km · 2-3 days

💡 Pro Tip

The Hanle night sky is one of the clearest on Earth — bring a tripod for astrophotography. The Indian Astronomical Observatory is visible from the homestays. BRO provides an official "Certificate" at the Umling La summit — frame-worthy! Start the summit day no later than 6AM for best weather conditions.
