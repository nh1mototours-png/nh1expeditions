Zanskar Valley

India's most remote valley. River crossings, gorge roads, and the cliff-cave Phuktal Monastery.

📏 ~1,400 km
📅 12–14 Days
☀️ Jul – Sep
🏔️ Max 5,359m (Khardung La)

Day-by-Day Itinerary

Into the Heart of Isolation

Click any day to expand details

⚠️ Zanskar Extreme Warning

  • ADV bikes / 4×4 ONLY — river crossings possible, avoid after rain
  • Carry minimum 20L extra fuel — stations almost non-existent in Zanskar
  • Padum has NO ATMs — carry ₹20,000+ cash minimum
  • Phuktal Monastery is 1.5hr trek each way from Purne — absolutely unmissable
▼
Day 1
Manali → Jispa | ~115 km
Acclimatization stop. Overnight Jispa (3,100m).
🏨 Jispa🏔️ 3,100m🛣️ ~115 km · ~4 hrs
▼
Day 2
Jispa → Sarchu | ~125 km
Baralacha La 4,890m. Overnight Sarchu (4,253m).
🏨 Sarchu🏔️ 4,253m🛣️ ~125 km · ~5-6 hrs
▼
Day 3
Sarchu → Leh | ~165 km
Tanglang La 5,328m. Arrive Leh 3,500m. Rest fully.
🏨 Leh🏔️ 3,500m🛣️ ~165 km · ~6-7 hrs
▼
Day 4
Leh Rest + Supplies + ILP | Mandatory
Full rest. Stock up on fuel, food, water, medicines. ILP permits at DC Office Leh. Zanskar has NO ATMs — carry sufficient cash.
🏨 Leh🏔️ 3,500m
▼
Day 5
Leh → Kargil via NH1 | ~230 km
Lamayuru Monastery — spectacular moonland. Mulbekh. Kargil (2,676m). Last good fuel stop before deep Zanskar.
🏨 Kargil🏔️ 2,676m🛣️ ~230 km · ~5-6 hrs
▼
Day 6
Kargil → Padum via Suru Valley | ~240 km
Sanku. Parkachik Glacier views. Rangdum Monastery — spectacular on a green plateau. Pensi La (4,401m) — gateway to Zanskar. Long winding descent. Padum (3,657m) — capital of Zanskar.
🏨 Padum🏔️ 3,657m🛣️ ~240 km · ~8-9 hrs
▼
Day 7
Padum Exploration — Karsha, Zangla, Stongde | ~60 km
Karsha Monastery — largest in Zanskar. Zangla fort and monastery. Stongde monastery (1,000+ years). Bardan monastery. Padum old town walk.
🏨 Padum🏔️ 3,657m🛣️ ~60 km · Full day
▼
Day 8
Padum → Phuktal Monastery | ~80 km + trek
Phuktal Gompa (3,900m) — built into a cliff cave, one of the most remote monasteries in the world. Drive to Purne village, 1.5 hr trek each way. Absolutely unmissable. This is the highlight of the entire trip.
🏨 Purne/Phuktal camp🏔️ 3,900m🛣️ ~80 km + 3 km trek
▼
Day 9
Padum → Nimmu via Zanskar Gorge Road | ~170 km
THE most challenging section. Zanskar gorge road (also the winter Chadar trek route). River crossings possible. Hanupata, Neraq. Zanskar river meets Indus at Nimmu — stunning confluence. Arrive Nimmu/Leh.
🏨 Leh🏔️ 3,500m🛣️ ~170 km · ~8-10 hrs
▼
Day 10
Leh → Nubra → Pangong (Optional) | ~250 km
Add Nubra and Pangong if time permits. Otherwise rest in Leh.
🏨 Pangong/Leh🛣️ ~250 km · Full day
▼
Day 11
Leh → Tso Moriri | ~220 km
Rupshu plateau lake. Overnight Korzok.
🏨 Korzok🏔️ 4,522m🛣️ ~220 km · ~6-7 hrs
▼
Day 12–14
Tso Moriri → Sarchu → Manali | ~470 km
Tso Kar. Pang. Tanglang La. Sarchu overnight. Next day Baralacha La, Rohtang, Manali. Day 14 is buffer for Zanskar road unpredictability.
🏨 Sarchu → Manali🛣️ ~470 km · 2 days + buffer
