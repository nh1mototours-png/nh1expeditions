Kashmir–Leh–Manali Grand

Dal Lake houseboats, Gulmarg gondola, the historic NH1, Lamayuru, and the full Ladakh circuit.

📏 ~1,800 km
📅 14–16 Days
☀️ Jun – Sep
🏔️ Max 5,359m (Khardung La)

Day-by-Day Itinerary

The Grand Himalayan Experience

Day 1
Manali → Jispa | ~115 km
Acclimatization start. Easy ride through Atal Tunnel into Lahaul Valley. Overnight Jispa (3,100m).
🏨 Jispa🏔️ 3,100m🛣️ ~115 km · ~4 hrs
▼
Day 2
Jispa → Sarchu | ~125 km
Baralacha La (4,890m), Suraj Tal, Gata Loops. Overnight Sarchu (4,253m).
🏨 Sarchu🏔️ 4,253m🛣️ ~125 km · ~5-6 hrs
▼
Day 3
Sarchu → Leh | ~165 km
Tanglang La (5,328m), More Plains. Arrive Leh. Mandatory REST.
🏨 Hotel in Leh🏔️ 3,500m🛣️ ~165 km · ~7 hrs
▼
Day 4
Leh Rest Day | Mandatory
Acclimatize. Leh Palace, Shanti Stupa, local market. Collect ILP permits.
🏨 Hotel in Leh🏔️ 3,500m
▼
Day 5
Leh → Nubra → Turtuk | ~170 km
Khardung La (5,359m). Hunder camels. Turtuk — last village near Pakistan border. Overnight Nubra.
🏨 Nubra/Turtuk🏔️ 3,048m🛣️ ~170 km · Full day
▼
Day 6
Nubra → Leh | ~120 km
Return to Leh via Khardung La. Prepare for the Srinagar leg of the journey.
🏨 Hotel in Leh🏔️ 3,500m🛣️ ~120 km · ~5 hrs
▼
Day 7
Leh → Kargil via NH1 | ~230 km
The historic Leh-Srinagar highway. Magnetic Hill. Lamayuru Monastery (spectacular moonland landscape). Mulbekh Maitreya rock carving. Drass — 2nd coldest inhabited place on earth. Kargil (2,676m). Overnight.
🏨 Kargil🏔️ 2,676m🛣️ ~230 km · ~5-6 hrs
▼
Day 8
Kargil → Sonamarg | ~190 km
Continue NH1 toward Srinagar. Zoji La Pass (3,528m). Sonamarg (2,730m) — the "Meadow of Gold". Sindh river. Stunning alpine meadows.
🏨 Sonamarg🏔️ 2,730m🛣️ ~190 km · ~5-6 hrs
▼
Day 9
Sonamarg → Srinagar | ~90 km
Arrive Srinagar (1,585m). Dal Lake houseboat stay — highly recommended. Evening Shikara ride on Dal Lake. Mughal gardens. Overnight houseboat.
🏨 Houseboat🏔️ 1,585m🛣️ ~90 km · ~3 hrs
▼
Day 10
Srinagar + Gulmarg Day Trip | ~60 km
Morning Dal Lake sunrise shikara ride. Drive to Gulmarg (2,650m) — world's highest golf course. Gondola cable car to 4,200m with jaw-dropping views. Return Srinagar houseboat.
🏨 Houseboat🏔️ 4,200m (gondola)🛣️ ~60 km return
▼
Day 11
Srinagar → Pahalgam | ~95 km
Pahalgam (2,130m) — Valley of Shepherds. Aru Valley, Betaab Valley (DDLJ filming location), Chandanwari. Lidder river. Amarnath Yatra base camp.
🏨 Pahalgam🏔️ 2,130m🛣️ ~95 km · ~3 hrs
▼
Day 12
Pahalgam → Patnitop → Jammu | ~230 km
Scenic drive through Pir Panjal foothills. Patnitop hill station (2,024m). Banihal Pass / Jawahar Tunnel. Overnight Jammu or Udhampur.
🏨 Jammu🛣️ ~230 km · ~6-7 hrs
▼
Day 13
Jammu → Dharamshala → Kullu | ~330 km
Optional Dharamshala/McLeod Ganj stop (Tibetan settlement). Or direct to Kullu/Mandi via the plains.
🏨 Kullu/Mandi🛣️ ~330 km · ~8 hrs
▼
Day 14
Kullu → Manali | ~60 km
Kullu Valley. Manikaran hot springs optional detour. Arrive Manali. Trip complete!
🏨 Manali🏔️ 2,050m🛣️ ~60 km · ~2 hrs
▼
Day 15–16
Buffer Days | Optional
Extra days for weather delays, extended Kashmir stay, or Zanskar detour from Kargil.
🏨 Flexible

💡 Pro Tip

This route combines the best of Kashmir AND Ladakh. Dal Lake houseboat overnight is a must. Gulmarg gondola to 4,200m offers the most spectacular views in Kashmir. The NH1 Leh-Srinagar highway through Lamayuru moonland and Drass is a legendary ride. No permits needed for Kashmir for Indians.
