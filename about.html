About NH-1 Expeditions

Driven by passion, fueled by the mountains.

Who We Are

Welcome to NH-1 Expeditions, your trusted partner for premium motorcycle rentals and guided adventures in the Himalayas. Founded by passionate riders, we intimately understand the thrill of the open road, the challenge of high-altitude passes, and the profound beauty of navigating the world's highest motorable roads.

Based in the beautiful valley of Manali, we have been serving the motorcycling community for over 8 years. Our deep knowledge of the terrain, combined with our unwavering commitment to quality and safety, makes us the premier choice for Himalayan expeditions.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: To provide the safest, most reliable, and exhilarating motorcycling experiences across the Himalayas. We believe that riding is not just about reaching a destination; it's about the transformative journey, the enduring camaraderie, and the legacy of stories created along the way.

Why Ride With Us?

  • 🛡️ Impeccably Maintained Fleet: Our motorcycles are meticulously serviced by expert mechanics and thoroughly inspected before every single trip to ensure peak performance and reliability.
  • 🗺️ Expert Local Knowledge: We know the intricate Himalayan routes—from popular highways to hidden trails—like the back of our hands and provide invaluable insights to all our riders.
  • 🤝 Unwavering Support: From comprehensive pre-trip planning to 24/7 on-call roadside assistance during your journey, our dedicated team is always there for you.
