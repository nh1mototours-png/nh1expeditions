Our Policies

Transparency is key. Read our booking, damage, and cancellation policies before you ride.

📋 Booking Policy

Eligibility

  • Riders must be at least 18 years old and hold a valid motorcycle driving licence.
  • Foreign nationals must carry a valid International Driving Permit (IDP), their home country licence, passport, and visa.
  • Learner licences are not accepted.
  • A minimum of 6 months of riding experience is required for high-altitude Himalayan routes.

Required Documents

  • Valid driving licence (original, with motorcycle endorsement)
  • Government-issued photo ID — Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, Voter ID, or Passport
  • 2 passport-sized photographs (for Inner Line Permits, where applicable)
  • Foreign nationals: Passport + Visa (original)

Payment Terms

  • A 30% advance is required at the time of booking confirmation.
  • The remaining balance + security deposit must be paid before vehicle handover.
  • Payment modes accepted: UPI, bank transfer, cash, and major cards.
  • Booking is confirmed only after both payment and document verification are complete.

Cancellation & Refund

  • 15+ days before pickup: 90% refund of advance amount
  • 7–14 days before pickup: 50% refund of advance amount
  • 3–6 days before pickup: 25% refund of advance amount
  • Less than 72 hours / no-show: No refund
  • If NH-1 Expeditions cancels due to unforeseen circumstances (weather, road closures), a full refund or rescheduling will be offered.

Rental Period & Late Returns

  • Rental period is calculated as 24-hour blocks from the time of pickup.
  • A 2-hour grace period is allowed for late returns.
  • Beyond 2 hours, a full additional day's rental will be charged.
  • Extensions are possible with 48 hours' advance notice, subject to availability.

Fuel Policy

  • Fuel is not included in the rental price.
  • The bike will be handed over with a full tank. Return with the same fuel level, or refuelling charges apply.

🛡️ Damage & Liability Policy

Security Deposit

  • A refundable security deposit of ₹10,000 – ₹25,000 is collected before handover, depending on the motorcycle model.
  • The deposit is refunded in full upon return of the bike in the same condition as received, with no outstanding charges.
  • Refund is processed within 3–5 business days via the original payment method.

Pre-Ride Inspection

  • A joint vehicle inspection is conducted before handover. All existing scratches, dents, or marks are documented with photographs.
  • We strongly recommend riders take their own photos/videos of the bike before departure.
  • The same inspection is repeated at return.

Minor Damages (Below ₹5,000)

  • Minor scratches, scuffs, or cosmetic damage below ₹5,000 will be deducted from the security deposit.
  • Normal wear and tear from riding conditions (e.g., dusty paint) is not charged.

Major Damages & Accidents

  • The rider is responsible for all repair costs resulting from damage, misuse, negligence, or accidents during the rental period.
  • Downtime charges apply — daily rental fees for the period the bike is unavailable due to repairs.
  • Repairs will only be carried out by NH-1 Expeditions authorized mechanics. Unauthorized repairs void the deposit refund.
  • In case of a major accident, the rider must inform NH-1 Expeditions immediately and cooperate with the insurance process.

Theft or Total Loss

  • In the event of theft or total loss, the rider is liable for the depreciated value of the motorcycle, including registration and insurance costs.
  • An FIR (police report) must be filed within 24 hours and a copy handed to NH-1 Expeditions.
  • Daily rental tariff may apply for an additional 30-day period while the claim is processed.

Insurance

  • All NH-1 Expeditions bikes carry mandatory third-party insurance as required by Indian law.
  • This does not cover personal injury or damage to the rented motorcycle.
  • We strongly recommend riders carry their own personal travel insurance covering medical emergencies and accidents.

⚠️ Usage & Safety Policy

  • Motorcycles are rented for personal use only — commercial use, racing, and stunt riding are strictly prohibited.
  • Riding under the influence of alcohol or drugs is strictly prohibited and will result in immediate rental termination without refund.
  • Helmet use is mandatory at all times. Quality helmets are provided with every rental.
  • The rider is solely responsible for all traffic violations and fines incurred during the rental period.
  • No unauthorized modifications are permitted on the motorcycle.
  • Any mechanical issues must be reported immediately to NH-1 Expeditions. Do not attempt repairs independently.
  • Taking the bike outside Himachal Pradesh or into restricted areas without prior permission is not allowed.
  • Pillion riders are allowed but must also wear a helmet. The rental agreement covers one primary rider only.

Last updated: February 2026. By renting from NH1 Moto, you agree to these terms.
