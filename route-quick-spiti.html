Quick Spiti Getaway

The perfect weekend warrior route. Manali to Kaza and back — every Spiti highlight in 4-5 days.

📏 ~400 km
📅 4–5 Days
☀️ Jun – Oct
🏔️ Max 4,590m (Kunzum La)

Day-by-Day Itinerary

Weekend Warrior Spiti

Click any day to expand details

▼
Day 1
Manali → Kaza | ~200 km
Early start at 5AM. Cross Rohtang Pass (3,978m) via Atal Tunnel or old road. Kunzum La (4,590m). Arrive Kaza (3,800m) by evening. Rest and acclimatize.
🏨 Kaza🏔️ 3,800m🛣️ ~200 km · ~7-8 hrs
▼
Day 2
Ki → Kibber → Langza → Komic | ~60 km
Full exploration day. Ki Monastery (4,166m) — the postcard shot. Kibber village, Chicham Bridge. Langza fossil village (4,460m) with Buddha statue. Komic (4,587m) — world's highest motorable village. Hikkim — world's highest post office.
🏨 Kaza🏔️ 4,587m🛣️ ~60 km · Full day
▼
Day 3
Kaza → Tabo → Dhankar | ~90 km
Tabo Monastery — UNESCO, 1000+ years. Dhankar cliff monastery overlooking Spiti-Pin confluence. Pin Valley entrance. Return Kaza for the night.
🏨 Kaza🏔️ 3,800m🛣️ ~90 km · Full day
▼
Day 4
Kaza → Chandratal → Manali | ~200 km
Detour to Chandratal Lake (4,250m) — the Moon Lake with mesmerizing blue-green waters. Kunzum La. Rohtang. Back Manali by evening. Or camp at Chandratal for the night and return Day 5.
🏨 Manali / Chandratal camp🏔️ 4,250m🛣️ ~200 km · ~7-8 hrs
▼
Day 5 (Optional)
Chandratal → Manali | ~80 km
If you camped at Chandratal, relaxed morning return to Manali. Trip complete!
🏨 Manali🏔️ 2,050m🛣️ ~80 km · ~3-4 hrs

💡 Pro Tip

This route is perfect if you have limited time but still want the authentic Spiti experience. Start Day 1 at 5AM to cross Rohtang before traffic. Chandratal camping (Day 4) is highly recommended — the starlit lakeside campsite is unforgettable. Kaza ATM is unreliable — carry ₹8,000+ cash.
