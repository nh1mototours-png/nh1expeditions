## 🏔️ Signature Routes

### 1. Manali → Leh (The Classic)
- **Distance:** ~479 km | **Duration:** 7–10 Days
- **Highlights:** Rohtang Pass, Baralacha La, Tanglang La, Nubra Valley
- **Best Season:** June – September

### 2. Spiti Valley Circuit
- **Distance:** ~850 km loop | **Duration:** 8–12 Days  
- **Highlights:** Kinnaur, Kaza, Key Monastery, Chandratal Lake, Kunzum Pass
- **Best Season:** Mid-June – October

### 3. Ladakh Full Circuit
- **Distance:** ~1,400 km | **Duration:** 12–16 Days
- **Highlights:** Pangong Lake, Tso Moriri, Khardung La, Hanle, Zanskar Valley
- **Best Season:** July – September

### 4. Zanskar Valley Ride
- **Distance:** ~600 km | **Duration:** 7–9 Days
- **Highlights:** Padum, Phugtal Monastery, Drang-Drung Glacier, Pensi La
- **Best Season:** July – September

### 5. Rajasthan Desert Run
- **Distance:** ~1,200 km | **Duration:** 10–14 Days
- **Highlights:** Jaisalmer, Sam Sand Dunes, Bikaner, Thar Desert Sunsets
- **Best Season:** October – March

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Do I need prior riding experience to rent a bike?**
> Yes. We require a minimum of 6 months of riding experience for Himalayan routes. For city/flat terrain bikes like the Suzuki Access, basic riding knowledge is sufficient.

**Q: What documents are required?**
> A valid driving licence (motorcycle endorsement), government-issued photo ID, and passport-size photographs. Foreign nationals must carry a valid passport.

**Q: Is a security deposit mandatory?**
> Yes. A refundable deposit of ₹10,000 – ₹25,000 is required depending on the bike. It is returned in full at the time of bike return, subject to condition check.

**Q: Can I ride to remote places like Spiti or Ladakh alone?**
> Absolutely! We offer self-guided tour packages with GPS route files, roadbooks, and 24/7 support. You ride your pace — we've got your back.

**Q: What happens if the bike breaks down on the road?**
> All expeditions include a support vehicle and an on-call mechanic. For rentals, we have a 24/7 roadside assistance hotline and network of partner mechanics at key points.

**Q: Is the Suzuki Access 125 suitable for mountain routes?**
> The Access 125 is best suited for in-town riding, flat terrain, and short scenic routes. We do not recommend it for high-altitude passes.

**Q: Can I extend my rental period mid-trip?**
> Yes, subject to availability. Contact us at least 2 days in advance to extend your booking.

**Q: Are helmets and riding gear provided?**
> Helmets are included with every rental. Additional riding gear (jackets, gloves, knee guards) is available on rent at nominal charges.

**Q: What is the best time to visit Manali and ride in the Himalayas?**
> June to September is ideal for Ladakh and Manali–Leh. Spiti is best from mid-June to October. Rajasthan routes are great from October to March.

**Q: Do you offer discounts for groups or long-term rentals?**
> Yes! Groups of 5 or more get a 10% discount. Monthly rentals come with preferential rates. Contact us to discuss.
